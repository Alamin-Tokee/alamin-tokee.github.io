abstract: >-
  Designed and developed a computer vision system to automatically detect refrigerator color, model, and door type on the assembly line, with real-time data capture and seamless database integration to streamline production tracking and reporting. The system significantly reduced manual inspection and human interaction, improving data accuracy and processing speed while enabling faster decision-making and process optimization through live production insights. This solution enhanced overall assembly line efficiency and supported Industry 4.0 smart manufacturing initiatives.
